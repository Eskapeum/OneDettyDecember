### Best Practices for Veo Prompts
1. Be specific about:
   - Camera angles (close-up, wide shot, aerial, etc.)
   - Lighting (golden hour, night, natural, etc.)
   - Mood/atmosphere (energetic, laid-back, etc.)
   - Duration (5-10 seconds per clip)
   - Quality (always specify 4K)

2. Include details about:
   - People (age, clothing, actions)
   - Location (Lagos, Accra, beach, club, etc.)
   - Colors and tones
   - Movement (slow pan, fast cuts, etc.)

3. Avoid:
   - Overly complex prompts
   - Requesting specific copyrighted content
   - Unrealistic scenarios

### Generation Tips
- Generate clips separately (easier to edit)
- Request 5-10 second clips (optimal length)
- Always specify vertical format (9:16) if possible
- Generate multiple variations and pick best
- Allow 2-5 minutes per clip generation
