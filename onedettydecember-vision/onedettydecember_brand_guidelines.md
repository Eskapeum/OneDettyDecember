# OneDettyDecember Brand Guidelines
## Unified Colors of Lagos & Accra

---

## 1. BRAND ESSENCE

### Brand Vision
OneDettyDecember is the definitive digital gateway to West Africa's most vibrant cultural celebration, unifying the electric energy of Lagos with the artistic soul of Accra into one seamless experience.

### Brand Mission
To connect the global African diaspora and cultural enthusiasts to the authentic Detty December experience through a platform that celebrates, preserves, and amplifies West African excellence.

### Brand Values
- **Unity in Diversity** - Bridging Lagos and Accra's unique identities
- **Cultural Authenticity** - Honoring tradition while embracing modernity
- **Vibrant Expression** - Bold, unapologetic celebration of African joy
- **Premium Experience** - Excellence in every interaction
- **Community Connection** - Fostering bonds across borders

## 3. TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Typeface: **Afrique** (Display)
- Headlines and hero text
- Celebrates African geometric patterns
- Bold, confident, and culturally rooted

### Secondary Typeface: **Montserrat** (Sans-serif)
- Body text and UI elements
- Clean, modern, highly legible
- Weights: Light (300), Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)

### Supporting Typeface: **Playfair Display** (Serif)
- Editorial content and premium communications
- Adds sophistication and storytelling quality
- Weights: Regular (400), Bold (700)

### Type Scale
- H1: 48-64px (Mobile: 36-48px)
- H2: 36-48px (Mobile: 28-36px)
- H3: 28-36px (Mobile: 24-28px)
- H4: 24-28px (Mobile: 20-24px)
- Body: 16-18px (Mobile: 14-16px)
- Caption: 12-14px

## 4. VISUAL ELEMENTS

### Logo Architecture

#### Primary Logo
- Combines a stylized "ODD" monogram
- Incorporates geometric patterns inspired by both Kente and Adire
- Features a subtle star element (Ghana's Black Star) integrated into the letterform
- Gold and black primary execution

#### Logo Variations
1. **Full Horizontal** - Complete wordmark with icon
2. **Stacked** - Icon above wordmark
3. **Icon Only** - For social media and app icons
4. **Monochrome** - Single color variations

### Pattern System

#### **Unity Pattern**
- Geometric design merging Kente strips with Adire circular motifs
- Used as background elements, borders, and decorative accents
- Scalable and modular for various applications

#### **Celebration Pattern**
- Abstract representation of festival energy
- Incorporates movement and rhythm
- Used for event promotions and dynamic content

#### **Heritage Pattern**
- Traditional motifs simplified for digital use
- Respects cultural significance while modernizing execution
- Used for premium and cultural content

### Iconography Style
- **Line Style**: 2px consistent stroke weight
- **Rounded Corners**: 2px radius for approachability
- **Grid**: 24x24px base grid
- **Two-tone Option**: Primary color with 20% opacity secondary

## 5. BRAND APPLICATIONS

### Digital Platform Design

#### Website Header
- Gradient overlay: Detty Gold to Lagos Pulse Red (15% opacity)
- Pattern overlay: Unity Pattern at 5% opacity
- High contrast text on imagery

#### Card Components
- White background with colored top border (4px)
- Subtle shadow: 0px 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Pattern accent in corner at 10% opacity

#### Buttons
- **Primary CTA**: Detty Gold background, Unity Black text
- **Secondary CTA**: Outlined, 2px border
- **Hover State**: 10% darker, slight scale (1.02)
- **Active State**: 20% darker, scale (0.98)

### Marketing Materials

#### Social Media Templates
1. **Instagram Posts**: Bold color blocks with pattern overlays
2. **Stories**: Vertical gradient backgrounds with animated patterns
3. **Twitter/X Headers**: Panoramic celebration imagery with logo placement

#### Event Posters
- Dynamic diagonal compositions
- Layer patterns at varying opacities
- Bold typography hierarchy
- QR codes integrated into pattern design

### Merchandise

#### Apparel
- Pattern placement on sleeves, collar, or hem
- Embroidered logos in metallic gold thread
- Subtle pattern watermarks on fabric

#### Accessories
- Pattern-wrapped packaging
- Gold foil stamping on premium items
- Coordinated color stories per collection

## 6. PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGERY

### Photography Style
- **Vibrant & Authentic**: Natural lighting, genuine emotions
- **Dynamic Angles**: Capture energy and movement
- **Rich Colors**: Enhance natural vibrancy without oversaturation
- **Inclusive Representation**: Diverse faces and experiences

### Image Treatment
- **Color Grading**: Warm tones, lifted blacks, vibrant highlights
- **Overlay Options**: 
  - Pattern overlay at 10-20% opacity
  - Gradient overlay for text legibility
  - Duotone treatment using brand colors

### Content Categories
1. **Celebration Moments** - Parties, concerts, festivals
2. **Cultural Experiences** - Food, fashion, art
3. **City Vibes** - Architecture, street scenes, landmarks
4. **People & Community** - Portraits, groups, connections
5. **Premium Lifestyle** - Hotels, restaurants, exclusive events

## 8. MOTION & ANIMATION

### Animation Principles
- **Rhythmic**: Movements inspired by Afrobeats tempo
- **Smooth**: 60fps for premium feel
- **Purposeful**: Every motion has meaning

### Signature Animations
1. **Logo Reveal**: Patterns form into logo (2s duration)
2. **Page Transitions**: Sliding with pattern wipe (0.3s)
3. **Loading Animation**: Pulsing patterns in brand colors
4. **Hover Effects**: Subtle scale and color shift (0.2s)

### Micro-interactions
- Button feedback
- Form validation
- Card reveals
- Navigation highlights

## 9. ACCESSIBILITY & INCLUSION

### Color Accessibility
- All text maintains WCAG AAA contrast ratios
- Alternative color combinations for colorblind users
- Pattern differentiation beyond color alone

### Typography Accessibility
- Minimum 16px body text
- Line height 1.5x minimum
- Clear hierarchy and spacing

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Respectful use of traditional patterns
- Accurate cultural representation
- Inclusive imagery and language
- Multiple language support (English, Pidgin, Twi)

## 12. USAGE GUIDELINES

### Do's
✓ Maintain color ratios
✓ Use patterns purposefully
✓ Respect minimum sizes
✓ Follow accessibility guidelines
✓ Celebrate both cultures equally

### Don'ts
✗ Stretch or distort logos
✗ Use patterns at 100% opacity
✗ Mix more than 4 colors per layout
✗ Use culturally inappropriate imagery
✗ Favor one city over the other
