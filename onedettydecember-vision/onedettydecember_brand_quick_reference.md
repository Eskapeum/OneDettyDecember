- Mix maximum 4 colors per design
- Keep patterns subtle (10-20% opacity)
- Maintain high contrast for text
- Balance both cities equally

### DON'T
- Use patterns at 100% opacity
- Forget accessibility standards
- Favor one city over another
- Mix competing brand colors
- Use culturally insensitive imagery
