# Omni-Scriptr Social Media Strategy

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the comprehensive social media strategy for Omni-Scriptr, a SaaS platform for AI-driven film scriptwriting. The goal is to build a strong brand presence, foster a loyal community, and drive user acquisition by establishing Omni-Scriptr as a thought leader in the scriptwriting and film-tech industry.

## 2. Goals & Objectives

Our primary social media goals are aligned with our business objectives:

*   **Brand Awareness:** Increase brand recognition and establish Omni-Scriptr as the go-to AI scriptwriting assistant.
    *   **Objective:** Grow our follower count from 0 to 600,000 across all platforms within 24 months.
    *   **Objective:** Increase brand mentions and shares by 25% quarter-over-quarter.
*   **Community Building & Trust:** Cultivate a vibrant community of writers, filmmakers, and industry professionals who trust and advocate for our brand.
    *   **Objective:** Achieve an average engagement rate (likes, comments, shares) of 3-5% on our content.
    *   **Objective:** Generate a consistent stream of positive user-generated content (UGC).
*   **Lead Generation & Conversion:** Drive qualified traffic to our website and convert followers into active users and paying subscribers.
    *   **Objective:** Increase click-through rates (CTR) from social media to the website by 20% quarter-over-quarter.
    *   **Objective:** Attribute a 15% increase in free trial sign-ups to social media efforts.

## 3. Target Audience

We will focus on three primary audience segments:

*   **Aspiring & Amateur Screenwriters:**
    *   **Pain Points:** Writer's block, difficulty with formatting, lack of industry connections, desire for feedback.
    *   **Goals:** Finish their first script, improve their craft, get noticed by agents or producers.
    *   **Content Preferences:** Writing tips, tutorials on script structure, motivational content, case studies of successful users.

*   **Professional Screenwriters & Filmmakers:**
    *   **Pain Points:** Tight deadlines, need for efficient collaboration, pressure to generate fresh ideas.
    *   **Goals:** Streamline their writing process, brainstorm more effectively, collaborate seamlessly with their team.
    *   **Content Preferences:** Advanced writing techniques, industry news and analysis, product demos showcasing efficiency and power-user features, discussions on the future of filmmaking.

*   **Indie Film Producers & Studio Executives:**
    *   **Pain Points:** Finding and vetting high-quality scripts, managing development pipelines, identifying new talent.
    *   **Goals:** Discover promising new scripts and writers, accelerate the development process.
    *   **Content Preferences:** Industry trend reports, articles on the impact of AI in film, success stories originating from the platform.

## 4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

We will track the following KPIs to measure the success of our strategy:

*   **Reach & Awareness:**
    *   Follower Growth
    *   Impressions & Reach
    *   Share of Voice (vs. competitors)
*   **Engagement:**
    *   Likes, Comments, Shares per post
    *   Engagement Rate (total engagements / total followers)
    *   Mentions & Tags
    *   UGC Volume
*   **Conversion:**
    *   Click-Through Rate (CTR) to website
    *   Social-Referred Traffic
    *   Conversion Rate (trial sign-ups, subscriptions from social traffic)
    *   Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) for paid campaigns

Success will be measured by our ability to not just grow numbers, but to build a community that actively engages with our content and advocates for the Omni-Scriptr brand.
