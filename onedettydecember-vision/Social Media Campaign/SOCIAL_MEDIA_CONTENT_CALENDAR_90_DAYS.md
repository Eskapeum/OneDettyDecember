## CONTENT GUIDELINES

### BRAND VOICE
- **Energetic** but not overwhelming
- **Authentic** and relatable
- **Inclusive** (Lagos AND Accra equally)
- **Cultural** but accessible
- **Fun** but professional

### VISUAL IDENTITY
- Use vertical color theming (Afrobeat Red, Coastal Emerald, etc.)
- Consistent fonts (Geist Mono, DM Sans)
- High-quality images and videos
- Detty Gold accent color
- Unity Black for text

### DO's
- Celebrate both Lagos and Accra equally
- Feature diverse voices (diaspora, locals, vendors)
- Use authentic Afrobeats music
- Highlight cultural moments
- Engage genuinely with community

### DON'Ts
- Don't be Lagos-centric
- Don't appropriate culture
- Don't overpromise features
- Don't ignore negative feedback
- Don't post without context

---

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### POTENTIAL ISSUES
1. Negative comments about platform
2. Cultural appropriation accusations
3. Lagos vs Accra rivalry
4. Vendor complaints
5. Technical issues

### RESPONSE PROTOCOL
1. Acknowledge within 1 hour
2. Investigate internally
3. Respond publicly with solution
4. Follow up privately if needed
5. Document for future prevention
